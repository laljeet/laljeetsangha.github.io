Laljeet Sangha is the Assistant Community Water Systems and Supply Advisor with UC Cooperative Extension, serving Kern, Kings, and Tulare counties. His work bridges drinking and agricultural water systems, emphasizing community resilience and sustainable water use.

He provides technical guidance on SGMA and ILRP, and collaborates with water managers, farmers, and agencies to address issues of drought, contamination, and groundwater planning.

Ph.D. – Virginia Tech | M.S. – Auburn University | B.S. – Punjab Agricultural University
